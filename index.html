ABOUT US

The KATHARSIS Wellness & Health concept was launched in 1998. It has been closely focused and applicable in the hotel offer since 2006, which makes it a pioneer in Montenegro when it comes to wellness offers.
Today, the KATHARSIS Wellness & Health concept is one of the best examples of an integrated and holistic approach to wellness services in the context of the hotel offer in Montenegro. It is based on the provision of classic high
quality services (various types of massages, aesthetic treatments, nutrition and physical activity) and on individually designed facial and body care programs (anti-aging, detox, slimming, etc.).
